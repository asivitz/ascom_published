p | Cloud Breaker is finally out!

| We had a grand release party, with a high score contest streamed to Twitch, and a delicious cake made by a few friends.

p Some early reviews are out, and the feedback is very interesting and helpful. This review, by 148Apps, is somewhat representative I think. I really had no idea people would find the game so difficult.

p I think for some players that is a user interface problem. The timer is a big part of the game– the villain if you will, but it doesn’t demand much attention. For new players, the Time Up screen seems to come out of nowhere. I submitted the first update today in an attempt to solve this problem.

p I cut a short gameplay video showing the changes. (You can also see some beautiful new sky colors. Yum!)

p Aside from that, I do think it can be a difficult game, but personally I think that’s a good thing. There’s a pretty long and gradual learning curve. The initial hump can be tricky, but if I can help players get over it, they’ll find a very satisfying progression.

p The 1.0.1 update should be out within a week, depending on the speed of Apple’s review queue.
