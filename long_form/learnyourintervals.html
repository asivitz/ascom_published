Learn Your Intervals

iOS App

This game will teach you musical intervals. For example, what is the perfect 5th of C? What is the major 6th of Bb? As you progress, it will test you on less common intervals and a tighter time limit.

It is made entirely in Haskell using my Hickory game library, using OpenGL for rendering and the reactive-banana library for state management.

It is currently beta testing. Contact me for details.
